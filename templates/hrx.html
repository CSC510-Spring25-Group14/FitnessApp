HRX Fitness

Inspired by Hrithik Roshan’s fitness journey, the HRX Workout is based on a strength training module.
The HRX Workout primarily focuses on your shoulders, quads, core, traps, and deltoid muscles. It is designed for all age groups and involves specific muscle training using weights and various movements.

It's a lifestyle choice that emphasizes self-improvement, resilience, and the pursuit of one's best self. The HRX program incorporates:

  • Primal Movements
  • Zero Momentum Reps
  • Compound Movements

At HRX, it’s our mission to motivate and enable you to work on your mind and body, ensuring you can become the best version of yourself. HRX is more than a brand; it’s a mission to support people in becoming the fittest, happiest, and most confident versions of themselves.
